Earlier this year I was discovering a lot of great new music in my <a href="/tag/my-indie-box/" target="_blank">In-die Box</a> posts. Although I discovered some great artists and songs, by far the best is Matthew Perryman Jones. His melodies are sweet and his lyrics are soulful. He&#8217;s an incredibly talented musician and I see him becoming big in the next couple of years. Sadly we missed him coming to town with Ingrid Michaelson earlier this year &#8211; it would have been an incredible concert!
